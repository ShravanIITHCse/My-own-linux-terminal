myps :
    Usage:
     myps [options]

    1) -e : Select all processes.

    2) -a : Select all processes except both session leaders (see getsid(2)) and processes not associated with a terminal.

    3) -F : Extra full format.

    4) -aF : Select all processes except both session leaders (see getsid(2)) and processes not associated with a terminal.Extra full format.
